대관자 유의사항

시설 사용 전 아래 유의사항을 반드시 숙지해 주시기 바랍니다.

  1. 1. Renter must be 25 years of age or older and provide proof of Identification upon signing rental agreement.
  2. 2. Damage deposit is required with signed rental agreement.
  3. 3. Liquor license and insurance for the event are required if liquor will be served.
  4. 4. Renter must restrict attendance to invited guests and accept responsibility for the conduct of all those in attendance.
  5. 5. Renter must restrict activities that may cause a fire, safety or health hazard.
  6. 6. The centre is a smoke free building. All smoking must be restricted to the parking lot on the south side of the building. The renter is responsible to clean-up used smoking materials after their event.
  7. 7. Renter may choose to be responsible for set up and clean up or may choose to pay for that service. Clean up requirements include: ・Stacking and storage of all tables and chairs. ・Cleaning of kitchen counters and mopping of kitchen floor. ・Sweeping of all floors including mopping of spills. ・Bagging of garbage and placement in outside receptacle. ・Removal of all decorations including tape. ・Clean the refrigerator and freezer.
